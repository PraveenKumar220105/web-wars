About Department

Engineering, science, and technology are influenced by society and, in turn, influence society. Engineering involves the application of knowledge to design, build, and maintain technologies. Our human values, needs, or problems often determine the research questions investigated and the problems engineers tackle. Meanwhile, the technologies that result from science and engineering influence society and change human culture.
