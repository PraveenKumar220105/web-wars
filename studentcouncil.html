About

Cmrcet CMR College of Engineering & Technology (CMRCET for short) is sponsored by the MGR Educational Society, which was established in 2002. The College is situated 20 Km away from Secunderabad Railway Station on the Hyderabad - Nagpur National Highway (Medchal Road), Telangana, India.
